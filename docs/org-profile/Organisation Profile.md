## Mission

Strengthening community wellbeing in Melbourne's Bayside region through practical support, connection, and food access programs.

## Overview

Southern Community Collective is a volunteer-led community organisation operating in Brighton and surrounding suburbs in the City of Bayside. Since 2011, we have delivered food access, social connection, and community wellbeing programs for local residents.

We support a growing number of residents on fixed incomes, older people, single-parent families, and renters experiencing housing stress — including those who may not access mainstream services.
